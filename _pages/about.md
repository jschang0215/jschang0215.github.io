I am an incoming Ph.D. student in Electrical Engineering at Stanford University. Currently, I am an undergraduate student in the Dept. of Computer Science and Engineering at Seoul National University. During my undergraduate studies, I worked on OS-level support for tiered main memory, under the supervision of Prof. Jung Ho Ahn. My current research focuses on hardware-software co-design and optimizing systems for emerging heterogeneous memory architectures.
