Juneseo Chang

Seoul National University. Computer Science and Engineering

I am an undergraduate student majoring Computer Science and Engineering at Seoul National University. I am was an undergraduate research intern at SCALable Computer Architecture Laboratory, Seoul National University, led by Prof. Jung Ho Ahn. I have also been advised by Prof. Daejin Park. My research interests include machine learning for system and memory management.

I am currently serving the mandatory military service (ROK DCC 2024.07 - 2026.01).
